• Bread Pakora

    Ingredients:

  1. Bread Slices:
  2. Besan (Gram Flour):
  3. Rice Flour:
  4. Red chilli,water,salt
  5. 1 cinnamon
  6. Baking Soda, Oil,
  7. Coriander Leaves
  8. Turmeric Powder
  9. Sugar or sweetener of your choice, to taste

    Recipe:

    Prepare the Batter:
  1. a mixing bowl, combine besan, rice flour (if using), salt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, asafoetida, ajwain, and baking soda. Gradually add water while stirring to make a smooth and thick batter. Ensure there are no lumps. Prepare the Bread Slices:
  2. Cut the edges of the bread slices (optional). If using potatoes, place a slice between two bread slices. Heat Oil:
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Dip Bread Slices:
  4. Dip each bread sandwich into the besan batter, ensuring it is well-coated. Fry the Bread Pakoras:
  5. Carefully place the coated bread into the hot oil. Fry until it turns golden brown and crispy on both sides. Ensure the flame is on medium heat to cook the pakoras evenly. Remove and Drain:
  6. Once done, remove the bread pakoras from the oil and place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil. Serve:
  7. Serve hot with green chutney, tamarind chutney, or any sauce of your choice. Optionally, sprinkle chaat masala and chopped coriander leaves on top for extra flavor
