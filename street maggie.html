2.Street Style Maggie

    Ingredients:

  1. pack of Maggi noodles (usually includes the noodles and the masala seasoning)
  2. 2 cups of water
  3. 1-2 tablespoons of cooking oil
  4. 1 small onion, finely chopped
  5. 1 small carrot, julienned or diced
  6. 1 small bell pepper (capsicum), diced
  7. 1/4 cup of green peas
  8. 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
  9. 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Chopped fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

    Recipe:

  1. Heat a large pot or deep pan over medium heat and add the cooking oil .
  2. Once the oil is hot, add the finely chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté them until the onions turn translucent and slightly golden brown.
  3. Add the diced carrots, bell pepper, and green peas to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  4. Now, add the 2 cups of water to the pot and bring it to a boil.
  5. Once the water is boiling, add the Maggi noodles to the pot. You can break the noodles into smaller pieces if you prefer shorter noodles.
  6. Stir the noodles and vegetables to ensure they don't stick together. Cook the noodles in the boiling water for about 2-3 minutes or until they become soft, stirring occasionally
  7. Once the noodles are cooked, reduce the heat to low and sprinkle the Maggi masala seasoning (included in the pack) over the noodles and vegetables. You can adjust the amount of seasoning based on your taste preferences.
  8. Mix the masala seasoning into the noodles and vegetables until it's evenly distributed.
  9. Taste the Maggi and add salt if needed. The masala seasoning usually contains salt, so adjust accordingly.
  10. Continue to cook for another 1-2 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld together.
  11. Remove the vegetable Maggi noodles from the heat, garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired, and serve hot.
